Founder Trustee Trustee
Dr. Radhike Khanna, Trustee and Consultant of Shraddha Charitable Trust is also the current Vice-Principal of The SPJ Sadhana School, Mumbai. She is a PhD in Special Education and holds a doctorate in Special Education from Knightsbridge University, England. She has done courses in Autism in Berard Method of Auditory Integration Therapy. Dr. Khanna has also completed a course in Public Engagement and attended the International Conference in Learning Disabilities, both at Harvard. She is the only Auditory Integration Therapist and S.S.Screener in the country. She has received numerous awards and recognition for her contributions, including the National Award for the best individual/professional in the field from our President, A.P.J. Abdul Kalam in December 2005 and NCPEDP-Shell Helen Keller Award 2008. Shraddha provides a means of livelihood and support for those that are often assumed to lead a dependent life.
Shraddha adopts a pioneering and creative approach where young adults with autism and developmental delay are encouraged
and assisted to take small steps towards self-sufficiency.
